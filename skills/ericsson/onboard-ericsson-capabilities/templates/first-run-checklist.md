# First Real Run Checklist

- **Capability:** [display name and stable ID]
- **Readiness state:** unknown-needs-check
- **Target:** [exact non-secret destination, issue, channel, mailbox scope, file, or
  workflow]
- **Intended effect:** [read, preview, draft, or specific change]
- **Reads:** [systems and bounded data categories]
- **Writes or changes:** [none or exact side effect]
- **Approval to execute:** [not required, not requested, pending, or explicitly authorized to execute this previewed action]
- **Output format and destination:** [format and confirmed new/safe destination]
- **Expected result and artifacts:** [result type, evidence, exclusions, warnings]
- **Inspection plan:** [how to confirm the result and detect partial effects]

Acknowledging the preview, approving its content, or approving a draft alone is not write authorization.
Do not execute a write while authorization is pending or the target/effect preview
is incomplete.
